REPLICA FINANCIAL APPLICATION

During a case competition, my group and I created Replica. The way it works is it is a personalized financial literacy app, that mirrors the financial situation of the user in the form of an AI. This AI gets simulations for the user to make decisions. Once the user makes the decision with their AI, they can see the results and understand what to do better in real life. The prupose of this app is to allow people to learn about finance better and for them to gain conidence in the world of finance.
